Assignment Workflow Summary

Typical Assignment Order:

  1. Create Groups, Subjects, Teachers, and Students (in other pages)
  2. Assign Teachers to Subjects (Section 1 above)
  3. Assign Subjects to Groups (Section 2 above)
  4. Assign Students to Groups (Section 3 above)

Result: Students will be able to see their subjects, and teachers will be able to enter grades for their assigned subjects.
